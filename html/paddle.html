All about paddleboards

Paddleboards are a bit like surfboards, but you stand up and paddle them.

Where to go

You can paddle in rivers, lakes, canals and the sea.

What do you need

  • A paddleboard
  • A paddle
  • A personal flotation device
  • A dry bag for your possessions

Where can I find out more?
